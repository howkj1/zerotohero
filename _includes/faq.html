Likes & Ledgers Event FAQ

What to know:

When does the event start?

Doors open at 8 AM.
The event will start promptly at 8:30 AM.
Be sure to allow for time to be parked, checked-in / registered, and seated by 8:30 AM.

Is there parking nearby?

- Studio205:
Limited parking is available directly at the venue.
- Street Parking:
You'll find additional parking on Tama Street, Benton Street, and 5th Street.
- Additional parking at South Story Bank.

Where should I NOT park?

Please Avoid:
Parking is NOT permitted at Casey's or the gravel lot located east of Casey's.

Will breakfast be available?

While a full hot breakfast will not be available, snacks, coffee, and water will be provided.

Will lunch be served?

Lunch is not included in the event.
After-party networking mixer details will be announced during the event.
Attendees are encouraged to support local area businesses.
Stay tuned for details!
