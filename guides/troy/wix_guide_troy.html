FAQ — 8 Questions to Add to Your Site

Add a FAQ section to your home page or a standalone FAQ page. These are the questions real visitors will have before they buy.

What happens right after I sign up?
You'll get a welcome email from Troy with a link to your member dashboard. From there you can access the video library, see upcoming group sessions, or book a private appointment — depending on which plan you chose. The whole process takes about 2 minutes.
How do I access the video content?
Through your member account on this site. Log in, go to your member area, and everything you have access to is right there. You can watch on your phone, tablet, or computer — no special app required.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, any time. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no runaround. Log into your account, go to My Subscriptions, and cancel with one click. Your access continues through the end of the billing period you already paid for.
What makes this different from other life coaching?
Most coaching focuses on goals, habits, and productivity. Those things matter — but they're incomplete without a foundation underneath. This coaching is built around the belief that God has a specific path for your life, and real progress means getting aligned with that path — not just optimizing around it.
Do I have to be deeply religious or attend church regularly?
No. This coaching is faith-based — meaning it's grounded in belief in God and the idea that your life has purpose. But you don't need to be a theologian or a regular churchgoer. If you believe and you're open, you're welcome here.
What if I join and it's not what I expected?
Reach out directly — Troy reads every message. The goal is for this to genuinely help you, not just hold your subscription. If something isn't clicking, let's figure out why before you go.
How do the group coaching sessions work?
Sessions are held live online via Google Meet. Troy posts the schedule each month. You join at the scheduled time, participate as much or as little as you're comfortable with, and can ask questions directly. Sessions are generally 45–60 minutes.
What if I'm not ready for a 4-week commitment — is there a way to start smaller?
Absolutely. The Basic plan ($30/month) lets you start with Troy's full video library and see if his approach resonates — no long-term commitment. The Intermediate plan adds live group coaching every month. The Advanced plan is for when you're ready to go all in: four weeks of private 1:1 coaching, fully focused on you.

Recording format — keep it simple

▶
Equipment: your phone is enough

A modern smartphone records excellent video. Prop it against something stable at eye level, or buy a $15 phone tripod from Amazon. No camera needed.

▶
Lighting: face a window

Natural light from a window in front of you (not behind you) is all you need. Daytime, curtains open. Free.

▶
Format: talk, don't read

Write 3-5 bullet points on a notepad, prop it just below the camera, glance at it if needed. Don't write a script — Troy's natural voice is his greatest asset. The more relaxed and conversational, the better.

▶
Length: 10-20 minutes per video

Long enough to go deep, short enough to watch on a lunch break. Don't pad it — end when you're done.

▶
Upload to YouTube as Unlisted, then link in Wix

Upload the video to YouTube → set to Unlisted (not Private, not Public) → copy the link → paste it into the Wix Online Programs lesson. This is free, fast, and keeps your Wix storage clear. See Phase 4 for the full steps.

▶
Same recording becomes a podcast episode

After recording a video, export just the audio and upload it to Spotify for Podcasters (free). One recording, two pieces of content. If Troy wants to build a podcast, this is how to start without extra work.

1
Get a reusable Google Meet link

Go to meet.google.com and click "New meeting" → "Create a meeting for later." Copy the link — it looks like meet.google.com/abc-defg-hij. This link is permanent and reusable — you can use the same link for every group session.
